W i l d L i f e

Sri Lanka is a small but facinating country filled with rich bio-diversity. We will help your to explore and uncover the nature of Sri Lanka. Our wild life tour package will cover three locations (Yala, Willpattu, Udawalawa) to give you the best wild life experience in SriLanka.

Willpattu National Park


The largest national park which located in the northwest coast in sri lanka, accommodating a large variety of tropical wildlife found in the island. The location approximately 188 km away from the commercial capital, Colombo. This is an excellent national park for nature lovers.

Yala National Park


Yala National Park is the most visited and second largest national park in Sri Lanka. Yala is famous for large number of Elephants, Leopards, Spotted Deer, Sambhur, Crocodiles, Wild Boar, Wild Buffalo, and many other animals.Also Yala's appeal that the main tracks and viewing spots can be crowded.

Udawalawa


Do you want to spot elephants in the wild? Then Udawalawe National Park is the best place in Sri Lanka. One of scenic national park in Sri Lanka, is located in Udawalawe. Massive land around 30 000 hectares, this park is home to so many different bird species,Crocodiles,Spotted deers,Lizards,Peacocks,Leopards,Buffalos and many more animals.


Our wild life tour package will cover the whole journey withing 5 days. Transport services, saftey and security are also included. A suitable tourist can be selected with the package.
